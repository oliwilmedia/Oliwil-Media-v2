About Us

Oliwil Media is a digital media company that focuses on delivering high quality content at a reasonable price to companies. The company was founded in May 2018 by Oliver Jonasson and William Slätt and is active throughout Sweden. The company strives to create long lasting and mutual relationships in order to create the best possible product tailored to the clients needs.
